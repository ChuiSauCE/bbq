WHO WE ARE

The BBQ Restaurants in Stratford and East Ham were established with our first venue in 1987 in the East End of London and have built up a reputation for the authentic Grill recipes, first class customer service and being a family friendly restaurant. The BBQ is a full-service restaurant catering for all Grill Food loving people. An authentic restaurant that takes pride in keeping the traditional recipes.
